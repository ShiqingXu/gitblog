> "One day," you said to me, "I saw the sunset forty-four times!"
>
> 
>
> And a little later you added:
>
> 
>
> "You know-- one loves the sunset, when one is so sad..." 
>
> 
>
> "Were you so sad, then?" I asked, "on the day of the forty-four sunsets?"

-- *Le Petit Prince*
